About Derby Duel

Derby Duel is a trivia game to test your knowledge on the infamous North London Derby

  • Answer correctly, goal scored! = 1 point
  • Win a match = 3 points
  • Draw a match = 1 point

  • Results after 3 matches:
    • 3 points or less - relegated!
    • 4 - 9 points - Premiership champions!
    • 10 - 18 points - Europa League winners!
    • 19 + points - Champions League winners!
